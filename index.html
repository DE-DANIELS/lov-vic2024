our love story

I am a fan of Love. And I am very open for Love, of course, true Love (1 Corinthians 13:4), which I have always prayed for. That day my immediate younger sister, Aunty Uche now Mrs. Lilian Eze mentioned that it would be pleasing to meet a beautiful young lady at her place of work.

And I affirmed.

Since I am in far away western Nigeria, Making a call is the quickest thing to do. Hence, I put a call through my younger sister, Mrs. Lilian Eze, and asked her to put her colleague on the call (I thought it was the normal usual man-Lady hello-hi-call)

As she picked the call and spoke up, we exchanged pleasantries. There is the beautiful voice with ultra calmness (unfaked), responses with great courtesy, touch of respect, and sprinkled with professionalism (Phone Etiquette) 16th November, 2022

I felt at home speaking with her and since then I have not stopped thinking of, discussing with, talking about and loving her. Imagine falling in Love with someone you have not met with one-on-one. It was as if the whole Love bank in me was opened for you, NWANKWO, LOVELYN IFEYINWA NKECHINYERE

We talked to each other through Mrs. Lilian Eze a couple of times and then I requested for her phone number. With this, we connected with all our social media platforms. Our first physical meeting was on Christmas 2022.

Our First date was Tuesday, 27th December, 2022. We communicate well; the good, the bad and the ugly.

By Sunday, April 9th 2023, our families were already aware of our decision to be together. Even though we live apart, we are as close as ever and each day, our Love grows. Frankly, our friendship has been good. And I found all I need in her. (NKECHINYERECHUKWUEBUKA)

I know God brought us together and through series of events in our lives, we have learnt our lessons and got prepared for the new life ahead together.

And we pray by His grace we will live till death do us part.

Through Christ Our Lord, Amen
